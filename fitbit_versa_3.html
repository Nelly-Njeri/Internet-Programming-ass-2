Fitbit Versa 3

Features

  • Always-On Display: Enjoy an always-on AMOLED screen for quick glances.
  • Built-in GPS: Track your outdoor activities without needing your phone.
  • Health Tracking: Monitor steps, distance, calories burned, and heart rate.
  • Sleep Tracking: Analyze sleep stages, heart rate during sleep, and restlessness.
  • Blood Oxygen Monitoring: Keep an eye on SpO2 levels during sleep.
  • Battery Life: Up to 6 days on a single charge.
